Registrations

FOR 2023 HONG KONG BLOCKCHAIN OLYMPIAD

All participants of any event affiliated with the Hong Kong Blockchain Olympiad must register with HKBCOL.

Registration for Training Workshops

The Blockchain Workshops are held from now until November 2023. Participants must register to obtain a certificate of participation from Hong Kong Blockchain Olympiad; those who do not register would not be able to get one.

REGISTER FOR TRAINING WORKSHOPS (G FORM)

If unable to access the form, please try using a virtual private network, such as Proton VPN.

Registration for Hackathon

The Blockchain Hacks (BCHX) is a weekend event for students to socialise, brainstorm, and kickstart projects ideas. It is an excellent opportunity to get more guidance on existing ideas or push a project past the procrastination barrier!

REGISTRATIONS TO BE ANNOUNCED AT THE WORKSHOPS IN JULY 2023

Registration for Examination

Students have a chance to test their knowledge and understanding of blockchain by taking the Hong Kong Blockchain Society Certification Exam 2023 Edition.
