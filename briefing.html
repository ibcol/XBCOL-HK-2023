HK Blockchain Society Workshops at AWS Summit 2023

Date: Tuesday, 23 May 2023
Time: 17:30 to 19:00
Venue: HK Convention and Exhibition Centre (S425, S426/7)

Video Recording of the Event [here].

Room 1:
Session 1: Crash Course in Cryptography and Blockchain Technology (20min)

  • Speaker:
    1. William Mui (HKBCS)
    2. Ken Chan (HKBCS)
  • Description: In this session, attendees will learn about the basic principles of cryptography and blockchain technology: why cryptography enables digital interactions and why blockchain enables a more accountable and trusted online experience.
  • Expected outcome: attendees gain a better appreciation and have realistic expectations of blockchain technology.

Session 2: Introduction to Launching Blockchain Infrastructure on Cloud (20min)
  • Speaker:
    1. Wing So (Amazon)
  • Description: In this session, attendees shall see how blockchain networks are setup on a cloud platform from scratch.
  • Expected outcome: attendees understand AWS as a platform for deploying blockchain applications.

Session 3: Introduction to Compliant Blockchain (Featuring ChatGPT) (40min)
  • Speaker:
    1. Harry Yang (HKBCS)
    2. Winnie Ip (HKBCS)
    3. ChatGPT
  • Description: In this session, attendees are encouraged to ask practitioners of blockchain solutions design on their experiences in building blockchain solutions for enterprise clients.
  • Expected outcome: attendees learn to be cautiously optimistic about blockchain solutions and applications.


Room 2:
Session 1: Hong Kong Blockchain Olympiad 2023 Briefing (20min)
  • Panel Speakers:
    1. Gabriel Chan (Moderator)
    2. Yalsin Li (HKU)
    3. Matthew Betty (CityU)
    4. Louise Chau (Lingnan)
  • Description: In this session, attendees are encouraged to ask past participants of Hong Kong Blockchain Olympiad programmes anything about their experiences from training to competing internationally.
  • Expected outcome: eligible attendees sign up for further training with the HKBCOL.

Session 2: How to transition from Web2 to Web3: a workshop for (potential) business owners (40min)
  • Speaker:
    1. Gabriel Chan
    2. Matthew Betty
  • Description: In this session, attendees are encouraged to ask practitioners of blockchain solutions design on their experiences in helping startups and giant conglomerates craft a realistic web3 and blockchain strategy.
  • Expected outcome: attendees learn how to make their businesses web3 ready.

Session 3: Introduction to Launching Blockchain Infrastructure on Cloud (20min)
  • Speaker:
    1. Wing So (Amazon)
  • Description: In this session, attendees shall see how blockchain networks are setup on a cloud platform from scratch.
  • Expected outcome: attendees understand AWS as a platform for deploying blockchain application.
