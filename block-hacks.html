Blockchain Sunday Sparks (BCSS)

The Sunday Sparks (BCSS) is an informal work session for current and recent students to socialise, brainstorm, and refine projects ideas. It is an excellent opportunity to get more guidance on existing ideas or push a project past the procrastination barrier!

DETAILS
