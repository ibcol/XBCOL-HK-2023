Blockchain Hackathon

The Blockchain Hacks (BCHX) is a weekend event for students to socialise, brainstorm, and kickstart projects ideas. It is an excellent opportunity to get more guidance on existing ideas or push a project past the procrastination barrier!

Date: 22-23 July 2023 (Saturday- Sunday)
Venue: University campus
Rundown:

  • Registration & Intermingling
  • Ice-breaking & group formations
  • Briefing of problem statements
  • Design Thinking Workshop
  • Lean Canvas Workshop
  • Business Solutions Workshop
  • Rapid Prototyping Workshop
  • Project Coaching Workshops
  • Open-Mic/Presentations
  • Peer/Expert Evaluations & Awards

About Us
