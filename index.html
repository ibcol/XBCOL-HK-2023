Welcome to the 6th Hong Kong Blockchain Olympiad
Bring Hong Kong into the Web3 Age

Competition Synopsis

Learn & practice hard skills demanded by the current market.

Design legal & compliant blockchain-enabled solutions.

Earn a chance to represent Hong Kong at the International Blockchain Olympiad finals in Amsterdam, Netherlands, EU.

HKBCOL invites teams of 1 to 6:

  • Current students studying at secondary or tertiary institutions or;
  • Recent student within 3 years of hiatus or graduation.

Why Join?

  • Future proof Skills for Self and Society

    Future-proof your skills and gain valuable experience in a field that is poised to grow rapidly in the coming years. Showcase your knowledge and abilities, network with industry experts and professionals, and stay up-to-date with the latest developments and best practices in the industry.

  • Certifications

    Come present and exchange ideas, and potentially gain recognition for your work! 8 teams will be shortlisted to represent Hong Kong and be guaranteed a place in the finals in Amsterdam. Top 3 will be presented with Gold, Silver and Bronze Medals. Distinction awards will be given to those who demonstrate exceptional knowledge and skills in blockchain technology, and merit awards will be given to those who show promise and potential in the field.

  • Gain Exposure

    Learn about the wide range of blockchain projects and applications at the Greater Bay Area Blockchain Olympiad, where teams from Hong Kong, Macau and China exhibit their work and share ideas.
