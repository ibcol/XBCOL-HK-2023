GBA IBCOL




About

The International Blockchain Olympiad (IBCOL) is an annual global competition, inviting students from all over the world to propose solutions for real-world challenges with blockchain technology. The GBA IBCOL is a regional competition amongst students in the Mainland of China, Hong Kong SAR and Macao SAR.

The teams with the best projects will receive funding for commercialisation of their product idea, along with the opportunity to compete in the IBCOL Finals taking place in October this year.

Form your teams:

Teams may be composed of 1 to 6 eligible individuals with one team lead, and each participant may join only one team. If you want to meet other participants, network, and learn how to draft a perfect pitch for the competition, please join our Ideathon event.

Proposals and formats:

Each team will identify a real world problem and come up with an overview of the solution using blockchain technology. The proposal should contain details of the solution architecture, and demonstrate how the solution addresses the problem. Participants can optionally include the following attributes in their proposals:

1. Market analysis
2. Competitive analysis
3. Revenue model
4. Distribution strategy
5. Development plan
6. Testing plan

The blockchain solution may be expressed with any myriad of tools, but at minimum, the following three P’s are required: (white) paper, poster (board), and pitch (deck). We highly recommend creating an elevator pitch and a recording of your pitch presentation. A prototype would be helpful: a low-fidelity (diagrams or mockups), high-fidelity (simulated application), or a functional prototype (with code).

It is not required to demonstrate technical skills — so no coding expected, let alone smart contracts or chain code — although if a team decides that it is useful for explanatory purposes, go for it!

Goodluck and may the odds be in your favour!
