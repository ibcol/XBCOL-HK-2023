Workshops and Resources

In addition to the main competition, HKBCOL also offers optional free workshops and training courses
to professionally develop your ideas and skills.

Blockchain Training

To support and prepare potential participants of IBCOL, there will be online courses running through April to November 2023. The courses will be delivered in two phases: beginner (April - July 2023) and advanced level (August - November 2023). Stay tuned!

Beginner Course
The Beginner Course will be conducted in English and will be made available as online resources for IBCOL. It includes the following 12 sessions:

  1. Introduction to Cryptography
  2. Introduction to Blockchain
  3. Current Events and Market Trends
  4. Decentralisation & Cryptography
  5. Decentralisation & Consensus
  6. Project Summary/Thesis Defence
  7. Edutech, Artstech, Contech, Proptech
  8. Fintech, Insurtech, Regtech, Suptech
  9. Initial proposal presentation
  10. Edutech, Artstech, Contech, Proptech
  11. Fintech, Insurtech, Regtech, Suptech
  12. Completed proposal presentation

Advanced Course
The Advanced Course will be conducted in English. To access online content of the advanced level, please send an enquiry to your Competition Committee or IBCOL. It includes the following 12 sessions:

  1. Privacy/Consent in Tokenisation
  2. Trust and Tokenisation
  3. Cryptocurrencies, Tokens, DeFi
  4. Payments, CBDC & Digital Fiat
  5. NFT Workshop
  6. Design Principles (PAC)
  7. SSI & SCI, DID, DKMS
  8. Case: Identity/Authentication Services
  9. Case: HKMA CDI, Digital Governments
  10. VC workshop
  11. Case: Clubs Membership System
  12. Case: Digital Logbook

Blockchain Training Certificate

Students can earn the following blockchain training certificates after passing the certification exams:

  • Certification Level 1 (Blockchain Fundamentals)
  • Certification Level 2 (Blockchain Solution Design)
  • Certification Level 3 (Blockchain Developer)
